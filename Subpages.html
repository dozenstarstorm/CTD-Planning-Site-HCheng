Website flowchart:

This is the overall layout of our wesbite.

Expectation of the look and feel:

  • Our webpage is giving out an ancient Chinese style impression through the background.
  • Our theme is to bring out the essence of the Chinese style photograpghy to light, making youngsters appreciate the art in it.
  • Each page is expected to include photographs to record the functioning of the studio and remind visitors the professionalism of this art.

Common structure of the pages:

  • Our page banner will be on each page. It will be on the same place(top right corner)and the same size.
  • We will bold the TIMES NEW ROMAN font for the headings and titles.
  • We will place the navigation bar below the logo and title.
  • The navigation bar will be on every page, so the visitors can have easy access and do not need to return to the main page every time.
