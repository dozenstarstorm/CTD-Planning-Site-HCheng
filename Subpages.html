Review:

  • Purpose: To create a site for students or photographers to know more about a traditional, antique photography studio, so they can consider of taking their own portraits in it.
  • Audience: We target photography students or amateur photographers because they would like to get in touch with traditional photography skills in order to develop their own skills.
  • Main message: The website is used for them to understand more background and services provided by this studio and most importantly give them the contact information for upcoming, plausible visits.

Website flowchart:

Expectation of the look and feel:

  • Our webpage is giving out a modern professionalism impression through the background.
  • Our theme is to bring out the modern style photograpghy to light, making youngsters appreciate the art in it.
  • Each page is expected to include photographs to record the functioning of the studio and remind visitors the professionalism of this art.

Common structure of the pages:

  • Our page banner will be on each page. It will be on the same place(top right corner)and the same size.
  • We will bold the TIMES NEW ROMAN font for the headings and titles.
  • We will place the navigation bar below the logo and title.
  • The navigation bar will be on every page, so the visitors can have easy access and do not need to return to the main page every time.
